The Great Genre Inversion: Comedy led, Drama caught, now dominates

In 2015, comedy produced 1.9× more titles per share point than drama. By 2021, drama crossed comedy for the first time (20.8% vs 20.1%). By 2025, drama holds 2.1× comedy's share — a complete structural reversal in a decade. The audience hasn't moved. The studios have.
