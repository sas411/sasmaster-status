Why this matters

The headline: a substrate layer was added between Strategy and Intelligence. Without it, every UI surface — portal-v8, v10, sasmaster-portal, War Room — was hand-coded one-off, and Moat #5 (Cinematic UI as Category Signal) lived as a hand-craft instead of an industrialized asset. Phase 1 closes that gap. The Substrate Layer translates Strategy §5.3 into reusable primitives that every agent in the Intelligence layer can render through deterministically.

The structural difference: decision-gate primitives turn Slack into the platform's control plane. !ready, !canon, !approve, !reject scope tightly to the four moments where human judgment beats agentic autonomy: prerequisite confirmation, canonical selection, audit sign-off, revision routing. Everywhere else, autonomous-coder runs unsupervised. This is the architecture pattern that scales — minimal human intervention, maximum agentic execution, deterministic decision gates.

What's next: the design system is now a living asset. Each phase formalizes more of the constitution into reusable substrate. By the time the Exchange is ready for buyers and sellers, the UI substrate underneath it will already be production-grade. Phase 1 doesn't just close the current gap — it compounds the cinematic UI moat over time.
