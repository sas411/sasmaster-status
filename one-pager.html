Unified Title Database
A master record of 589,000 movies and TV series, stitching together IMDB, TMDB, Nielsen, and Gracenote into a single, authoritative ID — the Parent Key. No other platform has this at this scale.
INTELLIGENCE LAYER
Autonomous Agent Fleet
10 specialized AI agents run 24/7 — collecting industry news, monitoring IAB standards, tracking technology advances, and synthesizing research briefs. Delivered to Slack before the workday starts.

Purpose-built interfaces for Programming, Marketing, Advertising, and CPG research teams. Each portal surfaces relevant data, audience analytics, and AI-generated insights for that specific buyer persona.
